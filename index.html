Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2026

Welcome to Dr.SD Admin. Your trust is our priority. This document explains how we handle your administrative data, shop details, and customer information within our platform.

1. Data Collection

  • Authentication Info: Email and UID managed via Google Firebase.
  • Shop Profile: Name, Address, Experience, and Business Categories.
  • Precise Location: Geographic coordinates to help customers find your shop.
  • Financial Data: UPI IDs and Payment QR images for your shop's transactions.
  • Media Assets: Profile photos and shop images stored securely on Cloudinary.

2. Purpose of Processing

We process your information specifically to:

  • Manage your tailoring business dashboard.
  • Enable order tracking and status updates.
  • Notify you of new orders and security alerts via OneSignal.
  • Promote your business location to users in the Dr.SD ecosystem.

3. Account & Data Deletion

⚠️ Right to Delete Data

In compliance with Google Play's User Data policy, Dr.SD Admin provides full control over your data. You may request the permanent removal of your account and all associated records at any time.

Option 1 (Automated): Use the "Delete Account" feature inside the App Settings for instant removal of all Database, Auth, and Cloudinary records.

Option 2 (Web Request): If you cannot access the app, use the form below to initiate a manual deletion by our technical team:

Open Data Deletion Form

*Note: Manual requests are processed within 24 hours. Once deleted, your business data and photos cannot be recovered.

4. Security Standards

Your data is protected using enterprise-grade security including Firebase Security Rules, SSL encryption, and device-specific session verification.
